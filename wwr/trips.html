Group Trips

This months featured trip is the group trips!! We have a lot of fun to offer for your large group, wether it be a large family reunion or a corporate get away for your office, you can count on us to have a great day set up for you here at California Grizzly White Water Rafting!
This month we hosted a several large group trips for thousands of the cast members from the worlds most magical theme park, Disneyland. This trip was full of a lot o adventure fun an laughs!
The cast members were lead in groups down on or our level III rapids (a medium intensity that is great for any one looking for an adventure!) They enjoyed the trip so much that many of them signed up to come back again as groups of friends and families!
We provided a catered lunch for all of the groups as we worked with some local restaurants who have many different options for all sorts of appetites. We also offered some outside games for people to enjoy as another way to spend a great time with friends!
We hope that you can also come and enjoy a great time with us on a large group trip. The best way to ask about pricing and what we can work out for your large group is to contact us today!

Family Trip

Need something fun to do with your family that isn't fighting over your tv set? Have we got something you are going to love! Our family trips range from group sizes of 3 to 20 people, including several rafts that can all go down the river together.
We have many different sorts of trips, from the most tame to the more thrilling for those who need more adventure in their lives. We are also happy to accommodate the same group in different ways depending on the age of the children and what they can handle! For more information about these trips, contact us today!

Couples Trip

Couples trips can be a great way to have a grand adventure with a close friend or significant other! Depending on what you want to do we can provide a tour guide and lunch as well! (prices vary by package). Contact us today for more information!
